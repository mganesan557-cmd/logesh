Real-World Use Case

See how our AI system prevented a potential data breach at a global tech company.

Preventing Data Exfiltration

A large enterprise was facing potential threats from internal users, both intentional and accidental. The company implemented our AI-powered insider threat detection system to analyze:

  • User activity logs
  • File access behavior
  • Privilege escalation patterns

The AI system identified an employee who began downloading large volumes of sensitive data during unusual hours. This behavior deviated significantly from their normal pattern of activity.

Security teams were immediately alerted through the system's dashboard, enabling them to intervene before any data exfiltration could occur. The entire process was handled seamlessly without disrupting normal business operations.
